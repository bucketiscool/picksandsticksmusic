Summer 2026

Kids Summer Band Camp

An immersive week where young musicians form real bands, make real music, and have an unforgettable summer.

🏕️ About the Camp

Our summer camp is designed for children who have some prior experience playing an instrument. We place each camper in groups by age and playing level to ensure the best experience for everyone.

The goal is simple: play together, make something original, and own it. Kids 10 and up are welcome.

  • 🎵

  • 🏷️
    Name Your Band
    Campers choose their own band name and create visual artwork to go with it.
  • 🤝
    Play as an Ensemble
    Learn what it means to listen, lock in, and make music as a real band — not just individuals.
  • 👦
    Age & Level Matched
    Campers are grouped by age and playing experience so everyone is challenged and supported.
